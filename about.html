About Maccesen

Macessen is dedicated to providing delicious, convenient, and ready to eat meals without compromision on quality. So its only a Small business with big heart, showing how passion in the kitchen can grow into something meaningful for the community.

Our story

Started during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, Macessen was born out of a desire to make food that are tasty and according to the what in season, yet still affordable and accessible to everyone.

Born from creativity and necessity, Macessen reflects how food connect families and communities even in hard times

From holiday celebrations to daily meal, the menu bridges tradition (nastar for lebaran) and modern tastes (Pasta, macaroni schotel) with a focus on fresh, high-quality ingredients and bold flavors.
